Features

Everything you need to remember what matters

Simple tools that get out of your way and let you focus on capturing the moment.

Voice Dictation

Speak your thoughts in any language. On-device speech recognition turns your words into text instantly.

Photo Memories

Attach photos from your library or camera. Each image is stored alongside your words, completing the story.

Smart Suggestions

Memoires detects unlogged trips and events from your photos, gently reminding you to write about them.

Mood Tracking

Tag each entry with how you felt. Ten expressive moods help you trace your emotional patterns over time.

Location Tagging

Automatically tag where each memory was written. See your entries on a map and revisit the places that matter.

iCloud Sync

Your journal travels with you. Sync seamlessly across all your Apple devices with end-to-end encryption.
